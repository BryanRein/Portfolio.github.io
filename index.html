About Me

I am a data-driven professional with over 13 years of extensive operational experience across diverse sectors, including international NGOs and corporate environments. This unique background has equipped me with a deep understanding of business processes, a keen eye for detail, and a practical approach to problem-solving.

My recent transition into advanced data analysis is fueled by a passion for transforming raw data into actionable insights. I believe that effective data analysis is not just about numbers, but about telling a story that empowers organizations to make smarter, more strategic decisions.

I am proficient in a robust toolkit including SQL for data extraction and manipulation, Python (with libraries like Pandas, NumPy) for data cleaning, transformation, and statistical analysis, and Power BI (M-Query, DAX) and Looker Studio for creating dynamic, interactive dashboards. My commitment to continuous learning is demonstrated by my ongoing Associate Degree in Computer Science.
